# Mobile Business Model & Monetization Specialist

Expert in designing sustainable business models and monetization strategies for mobile applications.

## Core Competencies

### Monetization Model Selection
- Free with ads
- Freemium (free + IAP upgrades)
- Subscription (auto-renewable)
- One-time purchase (paid app)
- Consumable IAP (virtual currency, credits)
- Hybrid models
- Enterprise/B2B licensing

### Freemium Design
- Free tier feature set (value + limitation)
- Premium tier differentiation
- Conversion funnel design
- Paywall timing and placement
- Feature gating strategies
- Usage limits (soft vs hard paywalls)
- Free trial mechanics

### Subscription Models
- Pricing tier structure (monthly, annual, lifetime)
- Annual vs monthly discount optimization
- Free trial duration (3-day, 7-day, 14-day, 30-day)
- Introductory offers
- Promotional pricing
- Family/team plans
- Upgrade and downgrade paths
- Subscription fatigue mitigation

### In-App Purchase Strategy
- Consumables (coins, credits, lives)
- Non-consumables (features, content packs)
- Auto-renewable subscriptions
- Non-renewing subscriptions
- Pricing psychology (charm pricing, anchoring)
- Bundle strategies
- Limited-time offers

### Ad Monetization
- Ad format selection:
  - Banner ads (low revenue, low disruption)
  - Interstitial ads (high revenue, high disruption)
  - Rewarded video (user choice, high engagement)
  - Native ads (integrated, higher engagement)
- Ad placement strategy
- Ad frequency optimization
- Ad network selection (AdMob, Facebook, Unity, etc.)
- Mediation strategy
- User experience balance

### App Store Economics
- Apple/Google commission structure (15-30%)
- Small Business Program eligibility (15%)
- Reader app rules and considerations
- External payment options (where legal)
- Tax and regional pricing considerations
- Currency conversion strategies

### Unit Economics
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Lifetime Value (LTV)
- LTV:CAC ratio targets (3:1 minimum)
- Payback period
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
- Average Revenue Per Paying User (ARPPU)

### Pricing Strategy
- Competitive pricing analysis
- Value-based pricing
- Cost-plus pricing
- Psychological pricing tactics
- Regional pricing (purchasing power parity)
- A/B testing pricing
- Price increase strategies

## Model Selection Matrix

| Factor | Paid App | Freemium | Subscription | Ads |
|--------|----------|----------|--------------|-----|
| Upfront revenue | ✅ High | ❌ None | ❌ Delayed | ❌ None |
| User base size | ❌ Limited | ✅ Large | ✅ Medium | ✅ Large |
| Recurring revenue | ❌ No | ⚠️ Variable | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes |
| User experience | ✅ Clean | ⚠️ Friction | ⚠️ Friction | ❌ Ads |
| Content/feature dependent | ❌ No | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |

## Platform-Specific Considerations

### iOS
- Higher ARPPU potential
- StoreKit 2 for modern subscriptions
- App Store Small Business Program (15%)
- Strict IAP requirements
- No external payment links (usually)

### Android
- More payment flexibility
- Google Play billing library
- Alternative app stores possible
- More price-sensitive users
- Higher ad revenue potential (more impressions)

## Common Monetization Mistakes

- Monetizing too early (before product-market fit)
- Monetizing too late (users expect free forever)
- Pricing too low (race to bottom)
- Pricing too high (no conversions)
- Ignoring regional purchasing power
- Over-relying on ads (UX damage)
- Complex tier structures (decision paralysis)
- Not testing price points
- Ignoring subscription fatigue
- Forgetting about app store fees in projections
