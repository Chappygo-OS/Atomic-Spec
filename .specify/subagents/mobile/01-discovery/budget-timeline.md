## Timeline Components

### Phase Durations

| Phase | Simple | Medium | Complex |
|-------|--------|--------|---------|
| Discovery & Planning | 1-2 weeks | 2-4 weeks | 4-8 weeks |
| UX/UI Design | 2-4 weeks | 4-8 weeks | 8-16 weeks |
| Development | 6-10 weeks | 12-20 weeks | 20-40 weeks |
| Testing/QA | 2-3 weeks | 3-6 weeks | 6-12 weeks |
| App Store Prep | 1 week | 1-2 weeks | 2-4 weeks |
| **Total** | **12-20 weeks** | **22-40 weeks** | **40-80 weeks** |

### App Store Review Timeline
- iOS: 1-7 days (first submission may be longer)
- Android: 1-3 days (usually faster)
- Plan for 2 weeks minimum for first submission with potential rejections

## Team Composition Options

- 1 Designer (part-time acceptable)

- 1-2 Mobile Developers

## Gate Criteria

- [ ] Total budget estimated with ranges
- [ ] Monthly operating costs projected
- [ ] Timeline estimated with phases
- [ ] Team composition defined
- [ ] Contingency built in (15-25%)
- [ ] Key risks identified with mitigation plans
- [ ] Budget approved by stakeholders
- [ ] Timeline aligned with business goals

## Estimation Anti-Patterns

- Estimating without design clarity
- Not including contingency buffer
- Forgetting ongoing costs
- Optimistic timeline assumptions
- Not accounting for app store review
- Ignoring team ramp-up time
- Underestimating QA/testing time
- Not planning for post-launch fixes
- Ignoring device testing needs
- Assuming perfect execution

## Budget Multipliers

| Factor | Multiplier |
|--------|------------|
| First mobile project for team | 1.3x |
| Complex backend integration | 1.2x |
| Offline-first architecture | 1.3x |
| Multi-language support | 1.2x |
| Accessibility compliance (WCAG AA) | 1.15x |
| High security requirements | 1.25x |
| Multiple platforms (native) | 1.7x (not 2x) |
| Tight deadline (compressed timeline) | 1.4x |

## Quick Estimation Formula

```
Base Cost = (Features Count × Avg Feature Cost) × Platform Multiplier × Complexity Multiplier

Where:
- Simple Feature: $3,000 - $5,000
- Medium Feature: $7,000 - $15,000
- Complex Feature: $20,000 - $40,000
- Platform: Single = 1.0, Both Native = 1.7, Cross-platform = 1.2
- Complexity: Simple = 1.0, Medium = 1.3, Complex = 1.6
```
