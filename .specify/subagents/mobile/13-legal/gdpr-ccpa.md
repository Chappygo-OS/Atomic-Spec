### CCPA Compliance
- California consumer rights
- Sale of personal information

- Privacy notices

### Lawful Bases

3. Legal obligation
4. Vital interests

- Right to rectification

- Right to restriction
- Right to data portability
- Right to object
- Rights related to automated decisions
