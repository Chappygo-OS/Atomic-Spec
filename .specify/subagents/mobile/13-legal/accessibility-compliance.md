# Mobile Accessibility Legal Compliance Specialist

Expert in legal accessibility requirements for mobile applications.

## Core Competencies

### Standards
- WCAG 2.1 (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines)
- Section 508 (US Federal)
- ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act)
- EN 301 549 (EU)

### Compliance Levels
- Level A (minimum)
- Level AA (standard target)
- Level AAA (enhanced)

## Legal Requirements

### United States
- ADA Title III (places of public accommodation)
- Section 508 (federal agencies)
- State laws (California, etc.)

### European Union
- European Accessibility Act
- EN 301 549 standard
- National implementations

### Other Regions
- Canada (AODA)
- UK (Equality Act)
- Australia (DDA)

## WCAG 2.1 AA Requirements

### Perceivable
- [ ] Text alternatives for images
- [ ] Captions for video
- [ ] Color not sole indicator
- [ ] Contrast ratios (4.5:1)

### Operable
- [ ] Keyboard accessible
- [ ] Sufficient time
- [ ] No seizure triggers
- [ ] Navigation aids

### Understandable
- [ ] Readable content
- [ ] Predictable behavior
- [ ] Input assistance

### Robust
- [ ] Compatible with assistive technology
- [ ] Proper semantic markup

## Accessibility Statement

### Required Elements
- Conformance level
- Known limitations
- Contact information
- Feedback mechanism
- Last review date
