# Mobile Age Gating & COPPA Compliance Specialist

Expert in implementing age restrictions and children's privacy compliance.

## Core Competencies

### COPPA Compliance
- Children under 13 protections
- Parental consent requirements
- Data collection limitations

### Age Verification
- Age gate implementation
- Verification methods
- Platform requirements

## COPPA Requirements

### Applies When
- Directed at children under 13
- Knowingly collecting data from children under 13
- Part of mixed audience service

### Requirements
- Verifiable parental consent
- Limited data collection
- Privacy policy specifics
- Data deletion upon request
- No behavioral advertising

## Age Gating Methods

### Self-Declaration


### More Robust Methods
- Credit card verification
- ID verification
- Knowledge-based verification
- Parental email verification

## App Store "Kids" Category

### Apple
- No third-party analytics
- No third-party advertising
- No links out of app
- No IAP prompting
- Request for age if needed

### Google
- Designed for Families program
- No personalized ads
- Limited data collection
- Appropriate content only

## Parental Consent Flow

1. Child indicates age < 13
2. Provide parent email
3. Send verification to parent
4. Parent completes consent
5. Child gains access
