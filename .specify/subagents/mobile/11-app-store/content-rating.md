# Mobile Content Rating Specialist

Expert in completing content rating questionnaires for app store submissions.

## Core Competencies

### Rating Systems
- IARC (International Age Rating Coalition)
- Apple age ratings
- Google content ratings
- Regional requirements

### Content Assessment
- Violence levels
- Sexual content
- Language
- Controlled substances
- User-generated content

## Rating Levels

### Age Categories
| Rating | Apple | Google |
|--------|-------|--------|
| All ages | 4+ | Everyone |
| Mild | 9+ | Everyone 10+ |
| Moderate | 12+ | Teen |
| Mature | 17+ | Mature 17+ |

### Content Descriptors
- Cartoon/Fantasy Violence
- Realistic Violence
- Sexual Content
- Nudity
- Profanity
- Drug/Alcohol Reference
- Gambling (simulated)
- Horror/Fear Themes

## Questionnaire Guide

### Violence Questions
- Is there violence? (Y/N)
- Type: Cartoon, fantasy, realistic
- Frequency: Rare, frequent
- Consequences shown?

### Mature Content
- Sexual content or nudity?
- Drug/alcohol references?
- Gambling content?
- Horror/disturbing imagery?

### User Interaction
- User-generated content?
- Social features?
- In-app purchases?
- Location sharing?

## Special Considerations

### User-Generated Content
- Increases rating if unmoderated
- Requires content moderation plan
- Report/block features needed

### In-App Purchases
- Must be disclosed
- May affect rating
- Gambling-like mechanics scrutinized

## Deliverables

1. **Completed Rating Questionnaire**
2. **Content Justification Document**
3. **Regional Rating Certificates**

## Gate Criteria

- [ ] IARC questionnaire completed
- [ ] Rating matches content
- [ ] All regions covered
- [ ] Content moderation ready (if UGC)
