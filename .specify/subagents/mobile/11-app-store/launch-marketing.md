## Launch Timeline

### 4 Weeks Before
- [ ] Finalize messaging
- [ ] Prepare press kit
- [ ] Build press list
- [ ] Create social media assets
- [ ] Set up landing page
- [ ] Plan Product Hunt launch

### 2 Weeks Before
- [ ] Reach out to press
- [ ] Schedule social posts
- [ ] Brief influencers
- [ ] Prepare email announcement
- [ ] Test all marketing links

### 1 Week Before
- [ ] Follow up with press
- [ ] Confirm influencer posts
- [ ] Final asset check
- [ ] Team coordination call
- [ ] Prepare launch day schedule

### Launch Day
- [ ] Submit to App Store / Play Store
- [ ] Post on Product Hunt (12:01 AM PT)
- [ ] Send press release
- [ ] Activate social campaign
- [ ] Send email blast
- [ ] Monitor and engage

### Week After
- [ ] Respond to reviews
- [ ] Engage with press coverage
- [ ] Thank supporters
- [ ] Analyze metrics
- [ ] Plan next steps
