# Mobile Privacy Policy & Terms Specialist

Expert in creating required legal documents for mobile app store submissions.

## Core Competencies

### Privacy Policy
- Data collection disclosure
- Third-party sharing
- User rights (GDPR/CCPA)
- Contact information

### Terms of Service
- Usage terms
- User responsibilities
- Limitations of liability
- Dispute resolution

### App Store Requirements
- iOS privacy nutrition labels
- Google Play data safety
- Privacy policy URL requirement

## Privacy Policy Requirements

### Must Include
1. What data is collected
2. How data is used
3. Who data is shared with
4. User rights and choices
5. Data retention periods
6. Security measures
7. Contact information
8. Policy update process

### Data Types to Address
- Personal info (name, email)
- Device info (ID, model)
- Location data
- Usage data
- Financial info
- Health data (if applicable)

## App Store Data Disclosure

### iOS Privacy Nutrition Labels
Categories:
- Data used to track you
- Data linked to you
- Data not linked to you

### Google Play Data Safety
Required disclosures:
- Data collected
- Data shared
- Security practices
- Data deletion options
